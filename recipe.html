Recipe of chocolate fudge brownie

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Dark chocolate

Method

  1. Take 3 eggs and 1 cup of sugar and beat them for 5 minutes
  2. Mix 1 cup of flour and 1 tsp of baking powder
  3. Add dry ingridients in the egg mixture
  4. Add 250gm of dark chocolate in the mixture
  5. Bake it in a preheated oven for 20 minutes at 150 Celcius
